INTRODUCTION

MentorMe Nigeria is a free platform that provides an avenue for young individuals (especially penultimate and final year undergraduates) to get mentored. Regardless of social and physical status, we believe every willing young individual should be able to access mentorship. On the platform, they access mentors and get paired with them for nine (9) months.

MentorMe Nigeria mentorship program is a comprehensive mentoring, designed to improve academic performance and develop professional skills and capacity. The program focuses on the healthy social, emotional, behavioural and professional development of the mentee.

The program affords mentors opportunity to pass on values and prepare capable hands that are necessary for the development of their ecosystem.

WHY MENTORING

Why Mentoring? Mentoring essentially provides young people with guidance and confidence to have strong footholds in their chosen careers. It gives them assurance needed to surmount day-to-day challenges and that they matter. Research affirms that quality mentoring relationships powerfully influence young people in a variety of personal, academic, and professional situations positively.

Mentoring shapes a young person to be positioned for social and economic opportunity, and it aids personal growth and development. However, one in every three young persons will grow up without this critical asset.

Roles played by mentors cannot be overemphasised. It makes a remarkable difference in the lives of youths. By acting as advisors, role models, and confidants to the students, helps them maximise their personal, academic and professional experiences and Identify how valuable they can be to their communities.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive program for young people to access quality mentoring relationships that offers guidance and opportunities in academic, professional, and entrepreneurship development.

Our Vision

To help young people attain desired growth and development in academics, professions and entrepreneurship through quality mentoring relationships

Overall Approach

To aid in capacity building of penultimate and final year students in the university, pairing them with established professional, proven entrepreneurs and academics, related to their chosen path.
