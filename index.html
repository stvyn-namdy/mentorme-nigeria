A Mentor-Mentee Program

Providing an empowering one-to-one mentoring relationships that helps young people from disadvantaged backgrounds breaking the inequality of guidance and opportunity in academic, entrepreneursip and professional developmennt

Who We Are.

MentorMe Nigeria is a free platform that provides an avenue for young individuals (especially penultimate and final year undergraduates) to get mentored. Regardless of social and physical status, we believe every willing young individual should be able to access mentorship. On the platform, they access mentors and get paired with them for nine (9) months.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive program for young people to access quality mentoring relationships that offers guidance and opportunities in academic, professional, and entrepreneurship development.

Our Vision

To help young people attain desired growth and development in academics, professions and entrepreneurship through quality mentoring relationships

Overall Approach

To aid in capacity building of penultimate and final year students in the university, pairing them with established professional, proven entrepreneurs and academics, related to their chosen path.

OUR PROJECTED GOALS

In the first phase of this project, 50 participants will be paired with 50 mentors for a period of 9months. Subsequently, we aim to reach a more significant number of youths, assist them in shaping their professional, academic and entrepreneurial goals. Youths who have mentors have a better advantage at professional and personal development.


Why Mentoring

Mentoring essentially provides young people with guidance and confidence to have strong footholds in their chosen careers. It gives them assurance needed to surmount day-to-day challenges and that they matter. Research affirms that quality mentoring relationships powerfully influence young people in a variety of personal, academic, and professional situations positively.
