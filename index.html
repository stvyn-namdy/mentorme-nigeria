Providing an empowering one-to-one mentoring relationships that helps young people from disadvantaged backgrounds breaking the inequality of guidance and opportunity in academic, entrepreneursip and professional developmennt

Who We Are.

MentorME Nigeria is a one-of-a-kind program that connects professinals from different industry (mentors) with Youths and students from low-income communities. Our aim is to bring these two close to each other and provide a platform for learning and growth.

OUR PROJECTED GOALS

The goal of this mentoring project is to involve at least 100 youths or students' in our first stage. Concurrently, we aim to enhance their understanding of their professional, academic and entrepreneurial goals by helping them naviagte through life's challenges. Youths with mentors have a huge advantage over a other youths from disadvantage backgrounds who doesn't have a mentor.
